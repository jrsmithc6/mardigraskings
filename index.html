About Us

Zachary Smith has been performing on the East Coast for more than 30 years. His group, the Mardi Gras Kings, brings a unique blend of zydeco, cajun, New Orleans jazz, and funk to every event. With a passion for delivering lively and unforgettable performances, we are dedicated to making your celebration truly exceptional.
